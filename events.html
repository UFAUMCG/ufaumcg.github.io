UFA|UMCG connects Cal Berkeley students with consulting and financial services professionals. We hold a number of signature annual events that are highly useful for both companies and students at the Haas School of Business and a variety of other venues. To host a corporate infosession with us, please contact our Co-Vice President of Investment Banking, Sahil Malhotra (sahilm@berkeley.edu).

Past Events

Summer in Investment Banking/Consulting Panel, we hosted an interactive panel discussion featuring senior students from prestigious firms including Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, Evercore, Bain & Co., and Simon-Kucher and Partners.
BAML Investment Banking Case Competition, we sponsored the 1st annual BAML case competition in partnership with the bulge bracket bank. The case offered students the opportunity to analyze the OpenTable IPO and present their findings.
Investment Banking Q&A Panel, representatives of Morgan Stanley, Bank of America Merrill Lynch, and Jeffries answered student questions related to investment banking and recruiting.
Real Estate Banking Panel with Morgan Stanley, MS professionals offered their thoughts on the real estate industry and the impact of the financial crisis.
Resume & Interview Workshop with Bank of America-Merrill Lynch, lead Merrill Lynch recruiter answered questions and presented techniques on how best to approach networking, resumes, and interviews.
Investment Banking vs. Consulting, professionals from both industries presented the unique aspects of both and offered advice on how students should approach networking and choosing a career in or the other.
Myth busters: Investment Banking Edition, Deutsche Bank professionals dispelled some of the commonly held misconceptions about the investment banking industry, spent time networking with students, and answered questions on culture and recruiting.
